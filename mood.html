About AI Mood Analyser

The AI Mood Analyzer is an interactive web application built using Streamlit that allows users to capture and analyze their emotions in real-time using their webcam. It leverages the DeepFace library to detect emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and more, providing a confidence score and an emoji representation for better visualization. The app also maintains a history of mood data, enabling users to explore their emotional trends over time through metrics, charts, and heatmaps. Users can filter mood data by date, view overall mood statistics, and download their data as a CSV file. Additionally, the app offers session management, customizable settings, and a visually appealing interface with a background image and responsive design. It is designed to provide insights into emotional patterns while ensuring ease of use and data persistence.
