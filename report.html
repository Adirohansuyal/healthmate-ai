About HealthMate AI

The Medical Report Analyser is an AI-powered application designed to simplify the interpretation of medical reports. It allows users to upload medical documents, such as images or PDFs, and provides detailed analysis, including key findings, diagnoses, and recommendations, using advanced AI LLMs like Gemini and Groq. The app also features an interactive Q&A system, enabling users to ask questions about the analysis and receive insightful responses. Additionally, it generates visual flowcharts to represent the diagnostic process and recommendations in a step-by-step manner. To further assist users, the app identifies relevant medical specialties based on the analysis and helps locate top specialists in a specified location, making it a comprehensive tool for medical report interpretation and specialist discovery.
